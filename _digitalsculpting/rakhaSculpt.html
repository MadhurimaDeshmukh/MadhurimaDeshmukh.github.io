Original Concept, sculpted using ZBrush. I was responsible for creating the character, Rakha in our game 'Empyrean'.
Set in a fantastic and mythical setting, Empyrean is a 3rd person shooter game where Rakha is an invader looking to steal the power from 'The Sun Kingdom'. Rakha's concept is inspired by Hindu and Buddhist cultures. I focused on creating a balance between his human-like body and his beast-like facial features.
Software used: ZBrush and Photoshop. Created in 2021.
