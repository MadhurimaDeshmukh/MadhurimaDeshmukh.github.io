Healthcare Without Boundaries

The Health Innovation at Kaiser Permanente website is built on WordPress. The CMS uses drag and drop modules allowing them to modify the layout and content of their website whenever their content needs to be updated. This system also allows them to build pages on their own without any development knowledge. The project also required working closely with their in-house illustrator to make all the hand-drawn images and SVG animations possible.
