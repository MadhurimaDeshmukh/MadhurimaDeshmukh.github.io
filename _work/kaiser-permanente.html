Healthcare Without Boundaries

The Health Innovation at Kaiser Permanente website is built on WordPress with Custom Post Types and Advanced Custom Fields. The admin uses drag and drop modules where they will be able to modify their website whenever their content needs to be changed. I also worked closely with their in-house illustrator to make all the hand-drawn images and SVG animations possible.
