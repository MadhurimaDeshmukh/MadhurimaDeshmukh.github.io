Guha Temple

Years ago, there was a meteor that crashed onto earth. This meteor had immense power, whoever touched it gained knowledge beyond any normal human. The first person who ever touched it was seen as the civilization's god, but one day they all just disappeared. What might have happen? Was this the meteor doing or was it the humans that did it to themselves? Find answers as you explore this cavern. Responsible for World Building; set dressing and lighting
