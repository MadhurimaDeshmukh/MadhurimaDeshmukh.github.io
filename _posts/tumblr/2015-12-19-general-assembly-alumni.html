General Assembly Seattle is over. I am now a web developer. My final project was Samesies. The project did not really have any requirements besides being a full stack application, so Samesies was built using the MEAN stack (MongoDB, Express, AngularJS, and Node.js). The idea of the project was to make a handful of templates and have them available to download for a small fee or for free all in one place.

The hardest part of the project was adding a password confirmation. I tried to add a custom directive, but I just was not getting it. I think if I had more time I would have been able to figure it out, but I took on a lot for a four day project. I ended up using a node module called angular-validation-match and used ng-show/ng-hide for a button. It may not be the best way, but it worked.
